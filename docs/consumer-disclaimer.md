# Consumer Disclaimer

This repository produces machine-readable research synthesis artifacts for evidence mapping, extraction, audit, and meta-analysis preparation.

The outputs are not medical advice, clinical guidance, or a substitute for reading the primary sources. Downstream consumers should preserve source identifiers, attribution, evidence maturity, provenance locators, review state, and known limitations.

Attribution and this disclaimer do not grant permission to copy, redistribute, or publish protected source expression. Artifact retention and export are controlled by `source_rights`, `source_snapshot.access_policy`, `text_snapshot` manifests, and the repository audits.

Public exports may contain citation metadata, structured facts, provenance locators, hashes, coverage state, synthesis-compatibility decisions, and rights metadata. They should not be treated as a public archive of article full text unless the matching rights record explicitly allows retained artifacts to be exported.
